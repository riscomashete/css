Admissions

Admission requirements and deadlines

  • Be a Namibian citizen or have permanent residence status in Namibia.
  • Have completed Grade 7 or equivalent from a recognized school.
  • Submit a completed application form along with the required documents, including a copy of the birth certificate, About transcripts, and proof of residence.
  • Meet the minimum About requirements for admission, which may vary depending on the program of study.
  • Pay the required application fee, which is non-refundable.
  • Submit the application before the deadline, which is usually in November or December for admission in the following About year.

Enrollment procedures

  1. Receive an acceptance letter from the school.
  2. Complete the enrollment form and submit it along with the required documents, including the acceptance letter, About transcripts, and proof of residence.
  3. Pay the required enrollment fee, which may vary depending on the program of study.
  4. Attend an orientation session, which is usually held before the start of the About year.
  5. Purchase the required textbooks and school supplies.
  6. Attend classes and participate in extracurricular activities as required by the school.
